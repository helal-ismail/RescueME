  • Pair device

    A User connects his wearable device with RescueME mobile Application via bluetooth then fill the prescreening survey by answering a few questions about his age, nature of work, daily routine and medical records and fill in the emergency contact list. Then make sure his device is charged, fastened well and let the data flow.

  • Analysis

    The device caputres key biometrics like heart rate and skin temprature and send them in real time to our analytics platform where they get analysed in real time.
    Data is tested against a set of patterns, each pattern represents a specific diagnosis like Arrythmia, Tachycardia and Bradycardia.

  • trigger alerts

    When a patient's data fall inside one of the patterns and the patient is diagnosed with a specific condition an Alert is triggered. The alert pulls the patient's contact list and notifies everyone in the list with the patient's condition via SMS, recorded phone calls and direct messaging like whatsapp, messenger and similar platforms.
